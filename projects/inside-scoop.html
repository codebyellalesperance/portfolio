Inside Scoop

Product Overview

An AI-assisted fashion trend tracker: save items to your closet, get weekly trend drops, and see look-alikes across retailers—in a clean, influencer-friendly UI.

The Problem

Trend discovery is scattered across TikTok/Pinterest, while pro forecasting tools are expensive. Shoppers want simple, actionable suggestions that match their saved items and budget.

Solution

  • Closet Library: Save/upload items or paste a product link—auto-pull image + metadata.
  • AI Matches: Color/pattern/shape embeddings match similar items across stores.
  • Weekly Trend Drops: Curated boards blending Vogue/runway/editorial + social signals.
  • Creator Mode (v2): Influencer tags, boards, and revenue-share rewards.

Architecture

  • Frontend: React + Tailwind, component library for cards/boards/filters.
  • Backend: API for product scraping + normalization; vector search for look-alikes.
  • Modeling: Image + text embeddings (colors, patterns, silhouettes) + trend weights.
  • Integrations: Retailer affiliate feeds (future), scheduled "Trend Drops."

Outcome & Next

  • Clickable MVP flows and brand system; early interest from creator friends.
  • Next: beta waitlist, creator dashboards, and price-tracking alerts.
